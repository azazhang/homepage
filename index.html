About the Artist

Welcome to the creative universe of JJJ_B - an independent music artist exploring diverse sonic territories without the constraints of traditional genre boundaries.

My work spans ambient, experimental, and electronic music, with recent releases including "Gloomy Vapor", "Knocking like a mess", "Fidelity on the Floor", and "Caught in the Trap".

While I don't typically perform live, I actively share my musical journey through performance videos. You can find me on YouTube as JJJB (channel: Studio J) and Bilibili, where I share my music-making process. This domain connects to my broader creative identity as azazhang.

Currently operating as a fully independent artist, I remain open to interesting collaborations and label opportunities.
