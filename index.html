About the Artist

Welcome to the creative universe of JJJ_B (also known as JJJB, Studio J, and azazhang) - an independent music artist exploring diverse sonic territories without the constraints of traditional genre boundaries. Across all platforms and identities, I craft original compositions that span multiple styles and influences, always prioritizing artistic authenticity over commercial expectations.

My extensive discography features numerous original releases including "Gloomy Vapor", "Knocking like a mess", "Fidelity on the Floor", "Caught in the Trap", and many more tracks that showcase my commitment to musical experimentation and creative evolution. Whether you know me as JJJ_B on streaming platforms or azazhang on various projects, the same creative vision drives all my work.

While I don't typically perform live, I actively share my musical journey through performance videos, where you can experience my music-making process. Currently operating as a fully independent artist under various creative aliases, I remain open to interesting collaborations and label opportunities.

The portrait you see here represents me across all platforms and creative identities - from JJJ_B to azazhang, this is my consistent visual identity.
