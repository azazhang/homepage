About JJJ_B

Welcome to the creative universe of JJJ_B - an independent music artist exploring diverse sonic territories without the constraints of traditional genre boundaries. I craft original compositions that span multiple styles and influences, always prioritizing artistic authenticity over commercial expectations.

My extensive discography features numerous original releases including "Gloomy Vapor", "Knocking like a mess", "Fidelity on the Floor", "Caught in the Trap", and many more tracks that showcase my commitment to musical experimentation and creative evolution.

While I don't typically perform live, I actively share my musical journey through performance videos on YouTube and Bilibili, where you can experience my music-making process. Currently operating as a fully independent artist, I remain open to interesting collaborations and label opportunities.

The portrait you see here represents me across all platforms and is my official visual identity as JJJ_B.
